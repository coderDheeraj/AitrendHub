Google has announced a new update to its AI-powered search experience, introducing a side-by-side browsing feature within AI Mode. The update is designed to make web exploration more interactive by allowing users to view websites alongside AI-generated responses.

This feature is currently rolling out on Chrome desktop and aims to improve how users search, compare, and interact with information online.

---

## What Is AI Mode?

AI Mode is Google’s conversational search experience that allows users to ask questions in natural language and receive contextual responses.

With this new update, AI Mode becomes more integrated with the browsing experience, enabling users to interact with both AI and web content simultaneously.

---

## Side-by-Side Browsing Experience

The most notable feature is the ability to open web pages next to AI Mode within the browser.

**How it works:**

- Clicking a search result opens the webpage alongside AI Mode  
- Users can continue asking questions without leaving the page  
- AI provides answers based on both the webpage content and broader web data  

This eliminates the need to constantly switch between tabs.

---

## Real-World Use Case

The feature is particularly useful for research and product discovery.

For example, when searching for a product like a coffee maker:

- You can describe your requirements in AI Mode  
- View recommended options instantly  
- Open a product page side-by-side  
- Ask specific follow-up questions such as usability or maintenance  

The AI then analyzes both the webpage and external sources to provide more detailed answers.

---

## Improved Focus and Productivity

According to Google, early testers responded positively to the feature.

**Key benefits include:**

- Reduced tab switching  
- Better focus while reading long articles or watching videos  
- Easier comparison of multiple sources  

This creates a more seamless and efficient browsing experience.

---

## New Tab-Based Search Integration

Google is also introducing a feature that allows users to search across multiple open tabs.

**How it works:**

- Access the “plus” menu in the search bar  
- Select recent tabs, images, or files  
- Combine them into a single AI-powered query  

This allows users to bring more context into their searches without manually copying information.

## Why This Matters

This update reflects a broader shift in how search engines are evolving:

- Moving from static results to interactive experiences  
- Integrating AI directly into browsing workflows  
- Enhancing productivity through contextual understanding  

By combining browsing and AI assistance, Google is redefining how users interact with information online.

Google’s side-by-side AI Mode feature represents a significant step toward a more integrated and intelligent web experience. By reducing friction and enabling real-time interaction with content, the company is making search more dynamic and user-focused.

As AI continues to reshape digital experiences, features like this could become a standard part of everyday browsing.
